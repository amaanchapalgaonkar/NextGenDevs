How It Works

To sell your carbon credits, please follow these steps:

  1. Enter your company details and the number of credits you wish to sell.
  2. Set a price per credit in USD.
  3. Upload valid proof of your carbon credits (PDF, image files).
  4. Submit for verification.
  5. Once approved, your credits will be listed in the marketplace.
